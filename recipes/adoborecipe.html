Adobo Recipe

Adobo is a Filipino dish of chicken or pork stewed in vinegar, garlic, soy sauce, bay leaves, and peppercorns

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thighs or pork belly
  • 1 whole head of garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp whole peppercorns
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 2 pcs bayleaf
  • 1/2 cup cane vinegar
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar

Cooking Steps

  • Wash chicken thighs or pork belly, drain and set aside
  • In a bowl, combine all ingredients and allow to marinade for 0.5 hrs, preferably overnight
  • Heat pan and add oil
  • Fry chicken or pork meat until brown or caramelized
  • Add the marinade mix
  • Simmer in low flame for 30minutes

    • Best served with hot rice
