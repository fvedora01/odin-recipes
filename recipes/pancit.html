Pancit Canton Recipe

For most Filipinos, It is not a birthday party if there is no pancit in the dish to be served. Pancit is a rice noodle dish of chinese origin. Typical pancit has choice meat, vegetables and cooked in

Ingredients

  • 1/4kg shredded chicken
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp whole peppercorns
  • 1 large onion, sliced thinly
  • 1 medium sized carrot,chopped thinly
  • 1/2 small cabbage, sliced
  • 1/4 kg french beans, sliced
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 liter water
  • 1/2 kg egg noodles (dry)

Cooking Steps

  • Wash chicken and set aside
  • Heat pan and add oil
  • Sautee onion and garlic.
  • Add peppercorns, chicken and sautee for 2 minutes
  • Add the vegetables and soy sauce
  • Add water, and let it boil
  • Add the egg noodles, mix
  • Simmer in low flame until noodles are cooked and almost all water have evaporated

    • Add toppings like egg, lechon pieces
