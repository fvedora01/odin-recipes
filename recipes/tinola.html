Tinola Recipe

Tinola is a Filipino ginger based soup dish made by boiling chicken with lots of ginger, some garlic cloves, peppercorns, onion and some green chili leaves. it is considered medicinal and is sometimes given to persons experiencing flu.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thighs
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp whole peppercorns
  • 4 thumbsize ginger, sliced thinly
  • 1 medium onion,chopped
  • 1/8 cup fish sauce, adjust to taste
  • 1 bunch chili leaves

Cooking Steps

  • Wash chicken and set aside
  • Heat pan and add oil
  • Sautee ginger, onion and garlic.
  • Add peppercorns, chicken and fish sauce, sautee for 2 minutes
  • Simmer in low flame for 30minutes, add chili leaves and serve hot

    • Add red pepper flakes to adjust spice level
